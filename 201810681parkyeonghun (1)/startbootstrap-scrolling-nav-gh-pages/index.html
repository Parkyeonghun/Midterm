About Kyrie irving

This webpage is a place to talk about Kyrie irving.He is famous for being the best dribble player in NBA. He is a player who has many fans because of his flamboyant style and steady progress. Although he is still a young player, he has made many achievements. This is his record.:

  • 2011–12 season: Rookie of the Year
    He also won the 2012 NBA Rookie of the Year Award with 117 of a possible 120 first-place votes.
  • 2012–13 season: First All-Star season
    Irving scored his then career-high 41 points against the New York Knicks.He became the youngest player in NBA history to score 40 points in Madison Square Garden
    The coaches selected Irving to play in his first All-Star game. Irving participated in the NBA NBA Three-Point Shootout and recorded 23 points in the final round to win the event.
  • 2013–14 season: All-Star Game MVP
  • 2014–15 season: Big Three formation and first NBA Finals
  • 2015–16 season: NBA Championship
    The Cavaliers finished the regular season as the first seed in the Eastern Conference with a 57–25 record. The Cavaliers won the series 4–3 and became the first team to rally from a 3–1 finals deficit, beating the defending champion Golden State Warriors to end a 52-year major sports championship drought in Cleveland.
  • 2016–17 season: Final season with the Cavaliers
